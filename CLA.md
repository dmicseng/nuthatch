# Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for contributing to Nuthatch. To clarify the intellectual property license granted with contributions, we require a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) on file for every contributor.

## Why a CLA

A CLA does two things:

1. **Confirms you have the right to submit the code** — you're not contributing someone else's copyrighted work without permission.

2. **Grants us a license to use your contribution** — specifically, it lets us (a) include your work in the AGPL v3 open-source distribution, and (b) offer the same code under a commercial license to organizations that can't use AGPL.

This dual-licensing model is what lets us keep the core product fully open-source while also offering a commercial option. Without the CLA, we couldn't legally do both.

Many projects in the same space use a similar CLA: [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/legal/contributor-agreement), [Plausible](https://github.com/plausible/analytics/blob/master/CLA.md), [Grafana](https://github.com/grafana/grafana/blob/main/contribute/developer-guide.md), [GitLab](https://about.gitlab.com/community/contribute/dco-cla/).

You retain the copyright to your contribution. The CLA grants rights — it doesn't transfer ownership.

## Individual CLA

By submitting a pull request, you agree to the following:

1. **You have the right to contribute.** The contribution is either your original creation, or you have sufficient rights to submit it under the terms of this agreement.

2. **You grant a copyright license.** You grant Nuthatch and recipients of software distributed by Nuthatch a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license to reproduce, prepare derivative works of, publicly display, publicly perform, sublicense, and distribute your contribution and derivative works.

3. **You grant a patent license.** You grant Nuthatch and recipients a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable patent license to make, have made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and otherwise transfer the contribution, where such license applies only to those patent claims licensable by you that are necessarily infringed by your contribution alone or by combination of your contribution with the project to which it was submitted.

4. **You confirm the contribution is your original work or you have disclosed third-party content.** If your contribution includes content from third parties, you'll identify it in your pull request description.

5. **Nuthatch may sublicense.** Nuthatch may re-license your contribution under different terms, including commercial licenses for organizations that cannot comply with AGPL v3.

This CLA is based on the Apache Software Foundation's Individual CLA, adapted for this project.

## Corporate CLA

If you're contributing on behalf of a company, your employer should sign a separate Corporate CLA. Email hello@nuthatch.io to request the template.

## How to sign

When you open your first pull request, the **CLA Assistant bot** will comment asking you to sign. You click a link, sign in with your GitHub account, and confirm. This records your agreement.

You only need to do this once. Future contributions are covered automatically.

## Questions

If you have questions about the CLA or want to discuss changes, open a discussion or email hello@nuthatch.io before submitting your PR.
